About me

Welcome to my website! I’m a Computer Engineering graduate from Roma Tre University, and I currently work as a DevOps Engineer. My academic background has given me a strong foundation in software development and systems architecture, which I now apply in building reliable, automated, and scalable infrastructure. This website is a space where I share both my academic and personal projects, reflecting the journey I’ve taken through university and into the professional world. Beyond my work in tech, I’m passionate about woodworking and analog photography—two creative outlets that allow me to disconnect, slow down, and build or capture things by hand. Feel free to explore my work, dive into my interests, and reach out if you have any questions or just want to connect. I’m always happy to exchange ideas and hear from fellow enthusiasts. Thank you for visiting!
