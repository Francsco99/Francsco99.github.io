    About Me

    Welcome to my website. I am a 24-year-old guy, graduated in Computer Engineering from Roma 3 University, and I am currently pursuing my master's degree in Computer Engineering. In addition to my passion for technology, I have a great love for analog photography. Experimenting with film and old cameras has become a hobby that I enjoy cultivating in my free time. In this online space, I will share with you my experiences, opinions, and knowledge on these two worlds that are so different yet fascinating at the same time. I invite you to explore my website and feel free to contact me if you have any questions or simply want to exchange ideas. Thank you for visiting!
